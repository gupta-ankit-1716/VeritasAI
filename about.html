The Digital Manifesto

Our commitment to forensic neutrality and the restoration of public trust.

01. Neutrality Protocol

VeritasAI operates as a "Zero-Bias" engine. We do not analyze content for political or social sentiment; we analyze for structural and neural inconsistencies. The truth is binary.

02. Privacy by Design

All forensic scans are processed in volatile memory. No media is stored on our servers past the analysis window unless explicitly saved to your secure history.

03. Transparent Confidence

We never provide a simple "Yes/No." Every verdict is accompanied by a confidence score and a frame-by-frame forensic log. We don't just tell you it's fake; we show you why.
